Core Team

We are hardly an year old, yet the kind of events that we have held in the Vizag ecosystem has kept us in the hotseats. Our team is small, yet the goal of all of us is to make the Vizag eco-system the next big developer hub with equal opportunity to women in tech.

The success of our events is only due to the unending support of our team which comprises of organisers, co-organisers and volunteers all of who work in their spare time to make our events as seamless as you see them.

The first prerequisite to be on the team is to be an attendee and be passionate about developer centric technologies. And if you feel you can contribute in any way, feel free to fill the volunteer form.
